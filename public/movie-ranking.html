The Best Movies According To Kane

My top 3 movies of all time.

Shooter

I love action movies but also love movies with a great story line with great depth to them. This was a favorite of mine as a young adult and has been ever since.

Angel Has Fallen

The best in the trilogy in my opinion. I am a sucker for Gerard Butler movies because he is such a phenomenal actor but this was definitely the best of the 3. Big action scenes, double crossing and supplemental actors helping to save the day!

Remember The Titans

What a phenomenal piece of history to remember! Came out when I was a kid and was a beautifully made movie! Awesome actors, great story line, and great morals of the movie!
